# 🛒 Zepto Inventory Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Zepto inventory data using SQL to derive meaningful business insights. The goal is to optimize inventory management, identify demand patterns, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze product availability and stock levels
* Identify high-demand and low-performing products
* Evaluate pricing and discount strategies
* Detect overstocked and understocked items

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about products, pricing, inventory, and sales.

### 🔑 Columns:

* **Category** – Product category
* **name** – Product name
* **mrp** – Maximum Retail Price
* **discountPercent** – Discount percentage
* **availableQuantity** – Current stock available
* **discountedSellingPrice** – Selling price after discount
* **weightInGms** – Product weight
* **outOfStock** – Stock status (0/1)
* **quantity** – Number of units sold

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* SQL (MySQL Workbench)
* CSV Dataset

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Inventory Analysis

* Identified out-of-stock products
* Detected low-stock items
* Analyzed stock distribution across categories

### 🔹 Pricing & Discount Analysis

* Products with highest discounts
* Average discount per category
* Maximum discount in ₹

### 🔹 Sales & Revenue Analysis

* Top products by revenue
* Category-wise sales performance
* High MRP but low sales products

### 🔹 Business Insights

* High discount but low sales products
* Low discount but high sales products
* Overstocked items (high stock, low sales)
* Products needing urgent restocking

---

## 📈 Sample Insights

* Some products had high discounts but low sales, indicating ineffective pricing strategy
* Certain categories showed consistent high demand with low stock levels
* Overstocked products highlight poor inventory planning
